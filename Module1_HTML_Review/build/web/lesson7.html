Method: Keep flowers long

  • Change water of the case every day
  • Rinse the case and pin before attaching
  • Avoid leaving the vase with sunshine, wind under the fan, on the windowsill
  • If the flower is dehydrated, use a spray bottle to spray the flowers
    Except flowers and white daisies must spray the flower back
  • Choose fresh flowers, dark colors, lush calyx, flower stalks are not broken
